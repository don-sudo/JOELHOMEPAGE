About Us

The The Baguio City National Science High School, operating under one System of Governance and Management,
provides scholarship to students with high aptitude in science and mathematics.
The BCNSHS System offers an education that is humanistic in spirit, global in perspective, and patriotic in orientation.

VISION
We are the leading science high school in the Asia Pacific Region preparing our scholars to become globally competitive Filipino
scientists equipped with 21st century skills and imbued with the core values of truth, excellence, and service to nation.
MISSION
The Baguio City National Science High School, operating under one System of Governance and Management,
provides scholarship to students with high aptitude in science and mathematics.
The BCNSHS System offers an education that is humanistic in spirit, global in perspective,
and patriotic in orientation. It is based on a curriculum that emphasizes science and
mathematics and the development of well-rounded individuals.
